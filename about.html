The Purpose of This Site

My name is Jesus, I made this site to let other people know(in this case some of my friends) about the good music I listen to. This site gathers almost all of those artists (hipop,rap,indie,lo-fi,allah,pop, like all genres sorta), some left aside because they are too mainstream, meaning that the people viewing this are likely fans or have heard music from those 'mainstream' artists. Nothing wrong with seemingly mainstream media, but the focus of this site is to expose those lesser known artists/bands. Surely some included in this site are far above from being underground(indie is the term right?) but those have been selected by myself and think of them as possibly honorable mentions. I could have just written a list on a text document and send it to people but thats boring.
